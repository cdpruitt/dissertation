the present treatment. The parameter labels correspond to those in the equations
of Chapter \ref{DOMFormalism}. For symmetric nuclei \oSix\ and \caForty, the
asymmetry-dependent parameters were disabled during fitting. As mentioned in
Chapter \ref{DOMResults}, for a few nuclei, one or more parameters were set to
zero to prevent the fit from drifting into unphysical territory, most notably the negative
imaginary surface and real wine-bottle parameters for \oSixEight.
